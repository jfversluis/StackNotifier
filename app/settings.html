StackNotifier

Get notified about your favorite tags on StackOverflow

I used to keep an open tab to StackOverflow and refresh it every once in a while. So, I decided to turn it into a Chrome extension so data will come to me instead of the other way around!
Simply add the tag(s) you want to be notified about separated by semicolons, determine a refresh interval and you're done!

Note: you are responsible for entering right values in the textboxes, there is no validation. Entering invalid values will lead to unwanted behavior.

This extension is created by Gerald Versluis and open-source on GitHub: https://github.com/jfversluis/StackNotifier
By logging in you can make more requests per day, this unlocks setting the interval manually
Tags that you want to get notified for. Can have multiple, separated by a semicolon.
Interval, in minutes, on which a refresh is executed. When not logged in a minimum of 15 mins is in place to not exceed the rate limit.
